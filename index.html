Credit Card Payoff Calculator

How It Works

Enter your credit card details below:

  • Current Balance: The total amount you owe on your credit card.
  • APR (%): The annual interest rate of your card.
  • Minimum Payment: Your card’s minimum monthly payment.
  • Target Payoff Date: The date by which you want your card paid off.

Click Calculate, and you’ll see:

  • The monthly payment required to reach your target date.
  • How much extra you’re paying above your minimum.
  • Total interest paid over the payoff period.
  • A chart showing how your debt decreases each month.
