3. Choose a Payoff Strategy

Two popular methods:

  • Debt Avalanche (Interest-First)
    • Pay extra toward the card with the highest interest rate.
    • Make minimum payments on other cards.
    • Pros: Saves money on interest.
    • Cons: Might take longer to feel progress if the high-interest card has a large balance.
  • Debt Snowball (Smallest-Balance-First)
    • Pay extra toward the card with the smallest balance.
    • Make minimum payments on others.
    • Pros: Gives psychological wins faster.
    • Cons: May cost more in interest overall.
