Pay More Than the Minimum

Even small extra payments reduce the principal faster, saving significant interest over time.

Snowball vs Avalanche

Snowball method: pay smallest debts first for motivation. Avalanche method: pay highest APR first to save the most money.

Negotiate Your APR

Call your credit card company. A lower APR directly reduces interest and shortens payoff time.

Automate Payments

Set up automatic monthly payments to ensure consistency and avoid late fees.

Track Your Progress

Use your dashboard and charts to visually track debt reduction and celebrate milestones.

Consider Balance Transfers

Transfer high APR balances to cards with lower interest to reduce overall cost. Be mindful of fees.
